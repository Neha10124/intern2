Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

1931 – 2015

"Dream is not that which you see while sleeping, it is something that does not let you sleep."

Biography

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Known as the "Missile Man of India" for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology, he played a pivotal role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998. A visionary, educator, and author, Dr. Kalam inspired millions with his humble beginnings, scientific achievements, and dreams for a developed India. He remained a youth icon till his last breath, even passing away while delivering a lecture at IIM Shillong in 2015.
